Take Action

How to take action:

It is super important to be aware of false information circulating the internet. If you see something incorrect, do not share it. If you want to post something, think before you post. Part of being a critical news thinker is being able to research a topic based on facts before sharing and being vocal. It is super important to be vocal about things that you have researched and are educated on.

Action Item List

Check off the boxes before you post or share

  • Step 1: Stop and think before you repost
  • Step 2: Look at the sources and information in a post or article
  • Step 3: Determine bias and plausible issues with the post
  • Step 4: If necessary look up more information or context
  • Step 5: If all seems credible, repost
